# <Mongoose-Playlist>
# <ins>SpotLab</ins>

A spotify collaborative playlist that allows signed in users to create playlists with their friends and family!  Users can add/remove songs and listen directly via the UI.

  Getting Started:
  
  1.) Use google credentials to login<br>
  
  2.) Navigate to the "ADD PLAYLIST" link and enter the playlists name<br>
  
  3.) Click on the "LISTEN" button to enter that playlist<br>
  
  4.) Add/remove/listen to songs freely by copying the embedded/pasting the embedded Spotify link<br>
  
  5.) Share your link with friends/family and collabate on your playlists!<br>
  
  [Click here to start listening!](https://mongoose-playlist-926.herokuapp.com/)
